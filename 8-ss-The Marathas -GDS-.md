1.  Describe the administrative reforms introduced by Shivaji. (1) Shivaji Maharaj created a
centralized, efficient administrative system to stabilize his kingdom. (2) He created the Ashta
Pradhana council to distribute responsibilities across finance, military, and diplomacy. (3) He
reformed local governance by appointing city kotwals to maintain law and order. (4) By
standardizing tax collection, he ensured the state had a consistent flow of funds.

the European practice of forcing Indian merchants to purchase navigation passes. (4) This policy
protected local merchant interests from exploitation.

strategy relied on extreme speed, surprise, and a granular knowledge of the rugged Deccan
terrain. (3) By avoiding direct open-field battles, the Marathas forced larger armies into attrition
campaigns. (4) This paralyzed the Mughal attempt to occupy the Maratha heartland.
