Section B — Short Answer Questions

Short Answer 1

Solve: 3(2x + 1) = 2(x + 5) + 7

3(2x+1)=2(x+5)+7 → 6x+3=2x+10+7 → 6x+3=2x+17 → 4x=14 → x = 3.5
Short Answer 2

The present age of A is 5 times B's age. After 10 years, A will be twice B's age. Find their present ages.

Let B = x. A = 5x. After 10 yrs: 5x+10 = 2(x+10) → 5x+10=2x+20 → 3x=10 → x=10/3... Let B=y, A=5y. After 10: 5y+10=2(y+10) → 3y=10 → y=10, x=50. B=10, A=50.
Short Answer 3

A two-digit number is 3 times the sum of its digits. If 45 is added, the digits are reversed. Find the number.

Let tens digit=x, units=y. Number=10x+y. 10x+y=3(x+y) → 7x=2y → y=7x/2. 10x+y+45=10y+x → 9x-9y=-45 → x-y=-5. Solving: x=2, y=7. Number = 27.
Short Answer 4

A train 300 m long crosses a pole in 15 s. How long will it take to cross a 150 m bridge?

Speed = 300/15 = 20 m/s. Distance = 300+150 = 450 m. Time = 450/20 = 22.5 seconds.
Short Answer 5

Divide ₹1200 among A, B, C so that A gets twice B and B gets twice C.

Let C=x. B=2x, A=4x. x+2x+4x=1200 → 7x=1200 → x=171.43. C=₹171.43, B=₹342.86, A=₹685.71 (or use 1:2:4 ratio: C=1200/7×1, B=×2, A=×4)
