Case Study 1: The Foundation of Swarajya

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (born 1630) founded a sovereign kingdom known as 'Swarajya'. At just 16
years old, he began capturing neglected forts, making them the core of his defensive state. To fight larger
empires, he perfected guerrilla warfare, which relied heavily on speed, surprise, and terrain knowledge,
famously seen when he defeated the Bijapur general Afzal Khan at Pratapgad. Shivaji Maharaj also
established a full-time Maratha Navy to protect coastal regions from foreign maritime powers.
