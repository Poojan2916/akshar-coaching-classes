💡 Smart Study Tips

📅 Plan Daily

Study 1 subject per day, 45 min sessions with 10 min breaks. Revision on weekends.

📖 Read Actively

Underline key terms as you read. Write 3 key points of every paragraph in your own words.

✍️ Practice Writing

For 3–5 mark questions, always practice writing. Reading is not enough — write it out!

🔄 Revise 3x

Read once → attempt MCQs → read again → write answers → final revision before exam.

⏱️ Time Yourself

MCQs: 1 min each. 3-mark: 4 min. 5-mark: 7 min. Practice with a timer every time.
