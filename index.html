Revision method

How to study smart

A simple routine that works better than long, unplanned study sessions.

Plan daily

One subject per day, 45-minute sessions with 10-minute breaks. Keep weekends for revision.

Read actively

Underline key terms as you read, and write three key points from every paragraph in your own words.

Practice writing

For 3 and 5 mark questions, reading is not enough — write the answers out by hand every time.

Revise three times

Read once, attempt the MCQs, read again, write answers, then do one final revision before the exam.

Time yourself

MCQs: 1 minute each. 3-mark answers: 4 minutes. 5-mark answers: 7 minutes. Always practice with a timer.
